Blue leads. Always.

Do use blue for headlines, links, primary buttons, the logo, and every moment that needs weight.

Don't mix blue and green in the same call-to-action. Green is a guest.

Cream, not white.

Do use cream as the default page background — it's what makes AGW feel like a painted room, not a browser tab.

Don't use pure #FFFFFF for body surfaces. Reserve white for the inside of cards and for photography.
