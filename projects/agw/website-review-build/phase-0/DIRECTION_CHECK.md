# AGW Direction Check

Date: 2026-04-14
Checkpoint: 1 of 3

## What we are validating
- Overall look and feel
- Trust level
- Estimate / booking direction

## Direction

We are taking AGW in a cleaner, more premium direction built around legacy, trust, and a smoother estimate path.

The site should feel:
- Established
- Polished
- Easy to trust
- High-quality without feeling flashy

It should not feel:
- Generic contractor
- Stock-photo heavy
- Discount-driven
- Overbuilt or busy

## Homepage direction

The homepage will be structured to do three things fast:
1. Establish trust immediately
2. Show AGW’s core services clearly
3. Move the visitor into an estimate request without friction

Planned homepage flow:
1. Heritage-led hero
2. Core service overview
3. Trust section
4. Simple estimate process
5. Reviews and local proof
6. Service area coverage
7. Final estimate CTA

## Trust direction

The strongest trust signals we plan to lead with:
- Since 1906
- Family-owned legacy
- Licensed and insured
- Full-time background-checked crews
- Warranty-backed work
- Clear communication and project management

## Estimate direction

We are planning a cleaner estimate flow that keeps the intake process simple:
- call or request estimate
- short form
- follow-up from estimator

The goal is to make AGW feel easier to hire without disrupting the current process.

## Asset direction

Using:
- approved logo assets
- approved brand patterns
- approved brand reference materials

Avoiding:
- ladder imagery
- risky jobsite visuals
- generic stock-photo people imagery

## Feedback needed

Please respond only on these three points:
- Does this direction feel like the right overall look and trust level for AGW?
- Does the homepage feel like it is emphasizing the right things?
- Does the estimate/contact direction feel correct?

We are not reviewing detailed copy, full page scope, or CMS structure at this checkpoint.
